Our Story

Where It All Started

Julmust Entertainment started as three friends in a rented apartment in Stockholm, arguing over what made games truly memorable. The answer we kept landing on: emotional stakes. Games that make you feel something real.

Our first title shipped to 200 players. Our third reached two million. We've never stopped asking the same question we started with — and that restlessness is baked into everything we make.

Today we're a studio of 60 across two offices, but the culture hasn't changed. Every voice matters. Every game starts with a pitch from anyone on the team. The best idea wins — always.
